OUR HIRING PROCESS

5 steps to join M+ Team

Apply

Visit our site and find a position you see to be the most appropriate for you. Send your CV

Interview

Whether we will hold the interview live or online, we will use the time to get to know you, introduce you to M +, provide more details about the position you applied for, and hear from you what your expectations are.

Introduction

When there is an opportunity, our Hiring team will review your CV, and if there is a match, we will contact you, and together we will set an interview time.

Decide

When your expectations and the position’s requirements are matched, we will contact you, and all you have to do is say “yes”

Language

If a position requires a foreign language proficiency, then during the interview, there will be an opportunity to talk with our language expert.

INTERVIEW TIPS

#beyourself

The job interview is probably the most important step you will take in your job search journey – it’s your best chance to show the company and hiring manager that you’re the best person for the job. Here are some job interview tips from our Hiring team.

Prepare

Before the interview, it would be great if you could use some time and find out more about M+ or our team. If we agree to have an online conversation, check your equipment and internet speed some minutes before.

Be on time

We recommend that you have everything ready a few minutes before the interview not to be overwhelmed by additional anxiety of losing time or the internet is not working right now.

Ask questions

Be open at any time during an interview and ask for everything that you find essential to you. That can be questions about details of the positions, the team or our company.

Be yourself

At every interview, we are trying to create a relaxed atmosphere. Be yourself and be open about all your interests, skills, and career expectations.
