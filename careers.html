WHY DO WE NEED TO YOU?

#teamplayer

We’re a team that provides support to various businesses by delivering a fantastic customer experience every day. We’re always looking for new team members who can share their experience, skills, and knowledge of foreign languages on the projects we do.

With each project, we want to create opportunities for everyone and use those opportunities to utilize a wide range of skills and talents.

Knowledge of foreign languages is not the only skill we develop. Cooperating with international clients, our teams have a great chance to show and acquire new knowledge, which allows us to deliver quality services to our clients from various industries.

OUR HIRING PROCESS

5 steps to joining M+

Apply

You can find all open positions and application forms on the website. If you need more info, we’ll provide you with everything you need to know to get a clear understanding of the job.

Interviews

Once you pass the language test, you will be joining our Hiring team and your future colleagues on the interviews to see if you are a good fit for your future team.

Introduction Call

Once your application has been reviewed, our Hiring Team will contact you with more information about the position, the company, and a quick interview.

Decide

You will get feedback on how your final hiring phase went. If you are the right fit for a position, we have an offer for you and we’ll give you enough time to evaluate.

Language Test

Depending on the role you’re applying for, you will get an opportunity to showcase your language skills on a written and/or verbal test.

INTERVIEW TIPS

#beyourself

The job interview is probably the most important step you will take in your job search journey – it’s your best chance to show the company and hiring manager that you’re the best person for the job. Here are some job interview tips from our Hiring team.

prepare

Make sure to learn a little bit about what we do, and read the job description thoroughly.

be on time

Always try to be there 10-15 minutes before the scheduled time, so you have enough time to settle and be ready.

ask questions

We are always happy to answer any questions you may have. We know how important finding out everything about the position can be for you.

be yourself

We encourage you to be yourself and show us all your values that would make you great for the position you applied for.
