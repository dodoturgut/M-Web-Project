Corporate Social
Responsability

RESPONSIBILITY IS OUR COMMITMENT

People are at the heart of our business, whether they are our employees, clients, or customers of our clients. Honesty and ethical business practices are at the core of our business. Starting from our employees and their safety and comfort, we spread the idea of caring for local communities and the environment through the countries and cities worldwide where we work and live.

Our focus will always be on building a healthy team environment, providing equal opportunities for everyone, encouraging inclusion, respecting cultures, and utilizing our diversity to support our clients and deliver exceptional services to their customers for overall success.

COMMUNITY


Given that we are an international and multicultural company operating in several countries and continents and supporting customers in more than 56 countries, diversity and synergy are the basis of our relationship with the community. We respect diversity and use it for our joint strength. We operate as a group, and therefore we can offer our clients a variety of expertise and knowledge and the 360˚ delivery platform for customer care support. Success comes with a sense of responsibility for our community, and authentic connection is being open and available to society with empathy, compassion, and goodwill, especially to those in need.

OUR EMPLOYEES


Starting from our employees, their safety, and comfort, we spread the idea of care. Customer care is a core of our business actions, and practicing care throughout all levels of our business ecosystem is our choice and commitment.

Our financial results and operational successes arise from our team's dedication, responsibility, and knowledge. Thus our employees are the fundamental comparative advantage of M+ Group and the most important source of our success. We are active participants in helping create sustainable jobs and value worldwide where we do business, positively impacting the communities.

As a company of people inspired by people, M+ Group is a melting pot for various nationalities, expertise, and generations. Our basics are inclusive selection and human management policies that promote equal opportunities and conditions for all employees in a healthy working environment in line with the requirements of our activity. With 57% of women in our workforce, diversity and non-discrimination are part of our corporate culture. At M+ Group, we value each interaction and create exceptional experiences for our employees, clients, and partners.

ENVIRONMENT


In terms of growing sustainability efforts at the M+ Group level, strong commitment towards creating better working conditions for our employees, a cleaner environment, and reducing our carbon footprint are of great importance. In the future, we plan to join some of the major global initiatives in the field to gain further momentum and apply best practices demonstrated by peers and companies from other sectors.

In line with the M+ Group's Policy on environmental and community impact risks, we are committed to acting responsibly in performing our procurement activities, considering the impact we may have on the environment. One of the objectives is continuous and thorough review and amendment of our supply chain policy to establish an environmentally friendly and sustainable supply chain framework that also includes social commitment as part of the selection criteria.

We are continuing our transformation process to boost evolution in our business and introduce greater flexibility while keeping a positive impact on our overall environment.

CORPORATE GOVERNANCE


As a business organization that operates and develops its business worldwide, we are aware of the importance of responsible and ethically correct behavior of business entities as a necessary prerequisite to developing quality relationships and fair competition between business partners. To that extent, we design and act according to good corporate governance practices and strive that our business strategy and policy, key internal acts, and business practices contribute to transparency and efficient business behavior, and better business relationships in the environment in which we operate.
